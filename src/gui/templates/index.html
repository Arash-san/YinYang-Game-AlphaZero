Game Rules

  1. Players take turns placing pieces on the grid
  2. Player 1 (Black) places black pieces, Player 2 (White) places white pieces
  3. All pieces of the same color must form a single connected group (horizontal or vertical connections only)
  4. No 2Ã—2 area within the grid should contain four pieces of the same color
  5. No complete row or column is allowed to contain pieces of only one color (both colors must appear in every filled row and column)
  6. The goal is to maximize the number of your own pieces on the board
  7. The game ends when no more valid moves are possible
  8. The player with more pieces on the board wins
